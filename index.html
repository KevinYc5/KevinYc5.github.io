About me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China, under the fortunate guidance of Prof. Chongxuan Li. Before that I earned my B.E. degree from the School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2023.
My research interests include deep generative modeling, with a current focus on diffusion models.
